Design Process

How I approach product design challenges

01

Discover

Understanding user needs, business goals, and technical constraints through research and stakeholder interviews.

02

Define

Synthesizing insights to define problems, create user personas, and establish success metrics.

03

Ideate

Exploring multiple solutions through sketching, wireframing, and collaborative design sessions.

04

Design

Creating high-fidelity designs, prototypes, and specifications for development teams.

05

Test

Validating designs through usability testing, gathering feedback, and iterating based on insights.

06

Deliver

Collaborating with engineers during implementation and measuring impact post-launch.
